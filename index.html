1. The Opportunity in Japan's EdTech Market

The GIGA School Program has created a once-in-a-generation shift.

Universal Device Access

Nearly every student in Japan now has a personal learning device, removing the hardware barrier.

High Demand for Software

With hardware solved, the focus has shifted entirely to finding effective, easy-to-use educational software.

The Problem with Competitors

Existing global platforms are often rigid, expensive, and not built for the specific workflow of Japanese educators.

2. The Kuizu Difference

We are not just an alternative; we are a better approach built on a teacher-first philosophy.

1.

Ethical & Accessible Model

Kuizu is free, with all core features available to every teacher. We are donation-based, not subscription-forced, eliminating the paywall barrier that restricts access to essential tools on other platforms.

2.

Tools for Deeper Learning

We go beyond simple right/wrong answers. Features like "Confidence Scoring" and "Explain Your Answer" prompts give teachers true insight into student comprehension, separating lucky guesses from genuine knowledge.

3.

A Complete Teacher Workflow

Kuizu is designed to save teachers time. With an integrated Lesson Flow builder, a personal Question Bank, and easy-to-manage Folders, our platform supports educators before, during, and after the lesson.

Innovative tools designed for deeper learning and greater efficiency.

Unlike other platforms that only show data from a single game, Kuizu provides a **Student Portfolio**. Teachers can view a student's progress over an entire semester, track their overall accuracy, and identify specific topics or question types where they consistently struggle. This long-term view is invaluable for personalized intervention.

Redemption Quizzes

Learning from mistakes is fundamental. After any quiz, Kuizu can automatically generate a **Redemption Quiz** for each student, consisting only of the questions they answered incorrectly. This provides an immediate and personalized opportunity to review and reinforce the material, prioritizing learning over a one-time score.

Interactive Lesson Slides

Kuizu allows teachers to build a full **Lesson Flow**, not just a game. They can create instructional slides with text and images, but more importantly, they can **embed interactive elements like polls or word clouds directly onto a slide**. This allows a teacher to pause during instruction, instantly gauge understanding or gather opinions, and then continue the lesson without ever leaving the presentation.

5. Conclusion & Future Roadmap

A commitment to continuous, teacher-focused innovation.

Conclusion

The Japanese EdTech market is ready for a new leader. By focusing on a teacher-first, donation-based model, offering tools for deeper learning, and respecting the complete teaching workflow, Kuizu is perfectly positioned to fill the gap left by existing global solutions. It is not just another quiz game; it is a comprehensive teaching and learning tool built for the unique opportunities of the GIGA School era.

Future Roadmap

  • Full Mobile Apps: Develop native iOS and Android applications using React Native for a seamless mobile experience.
  • LMS Integration: Integrate with popular Learning Management Systems like Google Classroom and Moodle for easy assignment and grade syncing.
  • Advanced "For Fun" Game Modes: Introduce new, highly engaging game modes like "Blook Rush" as a special reward or brain break for students.
  • Accessibility (a11y) Overhaul: A dedicated sprint to ensure the entire platform is fully compliant with modern accessibility standards for all users.
